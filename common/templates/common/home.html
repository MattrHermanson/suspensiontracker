"Set and Forget"

Suspension Tracker is a tool to save you time and make you faster.

Why I created
Suspension Tracker

I've always loved messing with my suspension setings and trying different things. I think this is the best way to learn what you like and why, but as it goes eventually you'll try something you don't like. Sometimes it's easy to just rewind the clicks you've just made, other times it isn't that easy. Many times I've gotten lost trying to work my setup back to where it was.

So after a few times ruining a good setup, I decided to make Suspension Tracker, an all in one tool to help you manage you bikes' setups and keep track of changes you are making. Suspension Tracker is designed to take the hard parts out of chaning you're bikes suspension setup. Hopefully you can utilize this tool to help you nail a setup that meshes well with your riding and helps you go faster.

- Matt
